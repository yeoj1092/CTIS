The revolution of artificial intelligence has a variety of advantages however, from an ethical perspective, there are a variety of concerns to think about as well. In an ethical perspective, questions that arise in relation to artificial intelligence more so relate come from whether or not it would affect human life in the near future. Questions such as the possibility of human workers being replaced by AI machinery, AI advancing to a point where machines start to develop emotions and gain rights of their own, and questioning the extent of trust we can have in these machines. If we don’t ask ourselves these questions, down the road this can have a negative impact for the future of our society.

Ethical concerns for society regarding artificial intelligence are presented in the role of human trust such as self-driving cars.
