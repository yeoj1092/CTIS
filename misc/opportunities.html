Technology Opportunities

The opportunities of Artificial Intelligence(AI) have provided the expectation of benefiting our society today. Over the years inventors and scientists have challenged machines with the ability to think. Whether machines can presently ‘think’ is now no longer surprising(Baecker,2019). Artificial intelligence technology has progressively become important and more demanding in creating new opportunities. We will be discussing how (AI) is applied to our daily lives as it is poised to dramatically improve efficiency and productivity.

Automation has had an effect with the reduction of human effort with the relations of machines programmed to carry out a task or job. As a result, automations can eliminate the need to perform tedious tasks considering the effect on work and jobs. Automated systems can be used to tackle such repetitive and replicated tasks. Many business owners already use a form of automations for example, companies such as Mitsubishi Motors who are the 9th largest worldwide by production, specialise in the automotive industry (Wikipedia,n.d). With their partnering company, Mitshubish electric has introduced “ LoadMate Plus “which is aimed towards manufactures as an automated machine tool that helps with labor. It is a task that removes parts, then mounts between two machines with the use of robotics(Illinois,2021). According to Hills (2021), Brodecki who is a services product manager at Mitsubishi Electric Automations mentions that automation solutions for machines heavily rely on mechanical robots and cobots for adjustability when it comes to performance and need of bigger parts. A prime example of (AI) use today is self driving cars. Elon Musk, co-founder and CEO of Tesla Motors has developed an autonomous electric vehicle which uses autopilot technology (Wikipedia,n.d). The technology they use is built on a deep neural network, with the help of cameras and sensors to guide the vehicle from your home to your destination. Functions within these Tesla vehicles include auto lane change, autopark, the feature to automatically r etrieve your vehicle as Tesla names ‘Summon’ and traffic light and stop sign control(Tesla,n.d).

(AI) can also be seen in the likes of education. Known as Education Learning, as students are given support outside of their classrooms for example, they are provided with online services. In depth, artificial intelligence programs are built to make intelligent tutors with the aim to support student learning such as identifying what students are struggling with and adapting tutoring solutions based on results(Baecker,2019). (AI) also opens opportunities for adults to carry on in education by providing them with automated admin tasks, systems to assess work, identifying skills and creating support systems(Schaffhauser,2020). In regards to automated admin tasks, it is noticed that educators spend a lot of time checking up on work, grading tests overall becoming overwhelmed with repetitive tasks. The process of applying automation software will save a significant amount of time. By doing this, it will completely remove much of manual paperwork(airSlate,2020). A significant example for the use of identifying skills, Carnegie Learning is a source that assists and enables educators to cater to all their students without having to focus on them individually (Carnegie Learning,n.d). This source provides service for students and educators to interact with tutors who can offer them a better and easier understanding on topics they may be struggling with. Fortunately for both students and educators, Carnegie Learning offers educational services for all subjects resulting in a broad service of identifying skills (Carnegie Learning,n.d).

(AI) is becoming more present in modern business and everyday life and it is also steadily being applied to healthcare. Although healthcare has progressed over time, there are still some global challenges mainly in the increased number of people in need of care. Global challenges include medical services for the elderly, creating medicine for new emerging illnesses and providing medical care for patients with long term health conditions (Ministry of Health,2018). Contrary to that issue, the help of AI and robotics healthcare is being transformed in many aspects. For instance, a health application and medical device named The Internet of Medical Things(IoMT) uses networking technology to obtain health care information in guiding patients to doctors in providing medical services and planning which can be more efficient and reduce costs (Joyia et al., 2017). Noticeably, technology applications and apps are helping individuals in providing knowledge to proactively manage a healthy lifestyle. In addition to healthcare, early detection software has been developed which helps predict symptoms or patients who are at risk of developing a condition. This will significantly help doctors to observe the most at risk to potentially develop life-threatening diseases (Griffiths,S.2016). This is only possible with machine learning to input extensive amounts of data to a well-defined output to practitioners with that leading down to decision making. Decision making is another area where AI is beginning to gain momentum in the field of medicine as it needs algorithms that can learn by themselves. The involvement of AI in this area helps by understanding the capabilities of physicians which require supercomputers(Mesko,2017).

Overall, the opportunities AI provides can be seen in automation, having it become very efficient with increasing productivity in businesses as well as advancing and developing towards education and healthcare making it useful and easy to understand. It is deemed revolutionary that machines can think and perform tasks the same way as humans seeing that it provides a lot of value to everyday life.
