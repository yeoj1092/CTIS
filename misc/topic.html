Artificial Intelligence

Since the world is rapidly developing in terms of technology, simple AI is becoming more common. In order to better develop AI, we need a stronger understanding of what an AI can do for us as a society. This website is meant to be a resource to help people understand not only the opportunities AI provides, but also the risks, and the choices we can make from those.
